When she steps away from her data, Margaret heads for a piano (Grade 6, Central Conservatory of Music) or a microphone — she once made it through the auditions for *The Voice of China*, which she still counts as one of her braver life choices. She is also always up for a game of badminton.

These are the things that keep her curious, a little competitive, and happily grounded. She finds that the discipline of practicing a piece until it sings, the nerve it takes to perform, and the quick footwork of a good rally all have a funny way of showing up in how she approaches research, too.
