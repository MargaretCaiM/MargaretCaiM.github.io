Published in *Scientific Decision Making* (科学决策), a CSSCI core journal hosted by the Chinese Society for System Analysis of Social and Economic Systems (ISSN 1006-4885; CN 11-3472/G3). Using nearly a decade of accounting data on China's A-share listed firms collected from the Wind and CSMAR databases, cleaned and processed in Stata, the paper builds an empirical model relating earnings structure to GDP forecasting, contributing empirical evidence on the link between accounting earnings and macroeconomic growth.
